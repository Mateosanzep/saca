This is the explanation of the jobs
Comunication
In this position you are going to make sure that all the people of the school find out about the activities. Both upload things on social networks or in the same WhatsApp groups.
Logistic
Your responsibility is to have a very good control of everything that is done. Know how much is spent on activities to have control. Ask permissions for anything. Knowing what activities can be done so that together with everyone it is organized.
Staff
You will have to assemble all the material that is used in the different activities.
Planner
Your job is to organize all the activities that are done. This implies taking out costs, materials, for what people, where and all these kinds of things.
Design
You are responsible for making posters, making flyers or doing anything else that is required for a design.
